About Mahadevapura

Mahadevapura is a major IT and industrial area located in the eastern part of Bengaluru. Known for its proximity to Whitefield and the Outer Ring Road, this region is home to several multinational companies and tech parks, including Bagmane Tech Park and Manyata Tech Park. The area has seen rapid urbanization, with numerous residential complexes, shopping malls, and restaurants coming up in recent years.

Key Places in Mahadevapura

  • Bagmane Tech Park
  • VR Bengaluru Mall
  • Phoenix Marketcity
  • Whitefield
  • Doddanekundi Lake
  • Krishnarajapuram Railway Station
