About South Bengaluru

South Bengaluru, known for its cultural heritage and green spaces, is one of the most popular and traditional parts of the city. It includes areas like Basavanagudi, Jayanagar, Banashankari, and BTM Layout, all known for their old-world charm blended with modern infrastructure. South Bengaluru offers residents a peaceful and balanced lifestyle, with access to parks, temples, markets, and educational institutions.

Key Places in South Bengaluru

  • Lalbagh Botanical Garden
  • Dodda Ganapathi Temple
  • Basavanagudi
  • Jayanagar Shopping Complex
  • Banashankari Temple
  • Ragigudda Anjaneya Temple
  • BTM Layout
