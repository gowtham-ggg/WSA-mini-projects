About Dasarahalli

Dasarahalli is a vibrant locality situated in the northwestern part of Bengaluru. Known for its blend of residential and commercial spaces, Dasarahalli is a bustling area with well-developed infrastructure. It features a mix of modern amenities and traditional markets, making it a convenient place for residents and businesses alike. The area is well-connected by road and public transport, ensuring easy access to various parts of the city.
