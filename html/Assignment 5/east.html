About East Bengaluru

East Bengaluru is a rapidly growing and vibrant region, home to some of the city's most important tech hubs, shopping malls, and residential areas. Key neighborhoods in this region include Whitefield, Marathahalli, Indiranagar, and Kalyan Nagar. East Bengaluru is known for its cosmopolitan lifestyle, with modern amenities, restaurants, cafes, and parks making it a popular choice for IT professionals and families alike.

Key Places in East Bengaluru

  • Phoenix Marketcity Mall
  • Indiranagar 100 Feet Road
  • HAL Aerospace Museum
  • Kempegowda International Airport
