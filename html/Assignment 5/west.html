About West Bengaluru

West Bengaluru is known for its industrial areas, bustling marketplaces, and growing residential localities. It includes key areas like Rajajinagar, Vijayanagar, and Malleswaram, which are rich in cultural heritage and historical significance. The region is home to traditional temples, educational institutions, and vibrant street markets, making it a popular place for families and professionals alike.

Key Places in West Bengaluru

  • ISKCON Temple, Rajajinagar
  • Orion Mall, Rajajinagar
  • Malleswaram Market
  • Sankey Tank Lake
  • Vijayanagar
  • Dr. Rajkumar Memorial
