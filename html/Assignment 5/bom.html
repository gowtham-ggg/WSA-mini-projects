About Bommanahalli

Bommanahalli is a vibrant locality situated in the southern part of Bengaluru. It is well connected to major hubs like Electronics City, Bannerghatta Road, and Silk Board, making it a preferred residential and commercial area. With a growing number of IT companies, schools, and shopping centers, Bommanahalli has become a bustling neighborhood catering to the needs of the modern-day population.

Key Attractions

  • Hulimavu Lake
  • Electronics City
  • Central Mall, Bellandur
  • Bannerghatta National Park
  • BTM Layout
