About RR Nagar

Rajarajeshwari Nagar, often referred to as RR Nagar, is a residential area located in the southwestern part of Bengaluru. Named after the famous Rajarajeshwari Temple, the locality has developed into a well-established neighborhood, offering a blend of modern amenities and green surroundings. RR Nagar is known for its peaceful environment, educational institutions, parks, and proximity to key parts of the city.

Places of Interest

Some popular places in and around RR Nagar include:

  • Rajarajeshwari Temple
  • Global Village Tech Park
  • Turahalli Forest
  • Innovative Film City
  • Bangalore University
